How I track my monthly spending

Every month I create a new Notes file on my phone where I write down everything I have spent money on. If it's in the 'Other' category I usually right a short description for it. So, currently I have these categories:

  • Groceries
  • Clothes
  • Beauty (moisturizer, body cream, nail polish etc.)
  • Eating Out (at work, on campus, with friends)
  • Other
I have created a Jupyter Notebook, where I have written a simple code to plot a pie-chart of my expenses (since my rent and insurance has been the same for over 3 months, I just add it to my expenses directly in the Notebook). I also visualize how my total spending is developing over time with a bar chart.
